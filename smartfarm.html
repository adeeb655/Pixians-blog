SmartFarm

Mobile Application

A smart Technology that empowers the indian
farmers by creating processed driven
ecosystem, which is both convenient and
sustainable.

Year

2020

Industry

Agriculture

What is Smart
Farm?

Smart farm intends to organize the unorganised
agricultural sector and empowers the Indian farmers
by creating a smart technology- enabled process
driven ecosystem, which is both convenient and
sustainable for them.

Farmers can purchase all the products needed for
farming and also sell their produce directly to
businesses through the app thereby eliminating any
losses they incur due to middlemen and brokers. This
effects the yield and cost.

Delivering the best services to
farmers all across the
country, with features and
services that help them
Grow...

USER INTERVIEW

usability for the technically challenged farmers. Having minimal knowledge of the market price of
their produce, unpredictable weather contitions, knowledge of sophisticated machinery &
equipment.

It was discovered that the farmers were not able to sell their produce to different markets.
