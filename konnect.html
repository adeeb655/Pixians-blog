Intro-Screens

Konnect has got almost all good features that any other networking appliciation has.
Through Konnect you can easily Sign In and get started.

We have added onboarding screen to assist the users with their initial steps in the app and make their journey more enjoyable with Konnect.

Group-listing

There is range of setting that enables you to manage and list groups in Konnect by locking, keeping them private and so on...

Video-call

The users can connect via Video calls and text messages like any other popular messangers.

The main thing is that the users can have a live chat while on a video call, record the call and add members at the same time.

Reminders

Reminders are important aspects that makes our life easy.

With this app you can set remainders of your meetings and calls anytime.

Set new remainders, get notified, add lables and
